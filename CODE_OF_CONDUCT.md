# Introduction

The **Genkit** community strives to maintain a welcoming, inclusive, safe, and
harassment-free environment. We'd like to ask every member of our community to
participate in creating this environment.

# Code of Conduct

We follow the
[Google Open Source Community Guidelines](https://opensource.google/conduct/).
Please read this document to understand the behavior we expect from our community
members.

# Reporting Guidelines

If you have a question or a concern not covered by the Community Guidelines, please
contact the project team at
[genkit-maintainers@google.com](mailto:genkit-maintainers@google.com).
